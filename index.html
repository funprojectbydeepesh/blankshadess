AboutUs

Stared as a hobby and love for design now turned into a profession. Visioned and started by Pramod Karki in Jan 2024. Beginning with uploading some basic designs in Instagram and slowly growing it to more of a visual dream. “Blank Shades” represents the shades we don’t reflect, or we try to hide which also plays a huge role in our designs and gives it a more artistic and contemporary feel as visioned by the founder himself. Started as one man dream now has a team with five members playing various roles. We create digital art and lay them in form of posters, merch and various other forms as per request.
